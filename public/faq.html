Frequently Ask Questions

Home / FAQ

Why do I need to register before posting a project?

As a project owner you need to register before posting a project for you to have a simplified process next time you want to post another project as you will not be required to submit again some common information.

How is the registration process for freelancers?

For a freelancer to be successful registered you need to subscribe to the system. The subscription costs only 6,500 Tsh and it lasts for a year. After subscription you will be able to access all available projects for the whole year.

How is the registration process for project owners?

For the project owners the registration process does not need any subscription. You will be free registered and after registering you can start posting your project at any time without any cost.

What do I have to do after my subscription is over?

After your subscription is over which will be a year since your last subscription you will need to register again and subscribe as a new member. The cost remains to be 6,500 Tsh per year.

Who is responsible for accomplishment of the project?

All project responsibilities after it is handled to freelancer including the accomplishment will be between a project owner and a freelancer. We recommend that the two parts should have a physical communication before carrying out the project.

Who do I pay after the project is successful accomplished?

After the project is successful accomplished the agreed payment amount between a project owner and a freelancer is paid directly to a freelancer.
